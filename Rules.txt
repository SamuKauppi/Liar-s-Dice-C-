RULES TO LIAR's DICE

- Each player has a cup that has 6 dice
- Each player can only see their own 6 dice

Bidding
- On their turn, player can bid on how many copies are there in total of a single dice 
(For Example: there are 6 copies of the dice 4)
- Player can only call the same amount or higher than the previous.
(For Example: if previous player called 6 copies of 4, the current player can only call
 6 or higher count)
- Player can't make the same exact call as the previous player and if the same count is called
 for an round, the starting player has to call higher count.

Calling
- On their turn, player can also call the previous player a liar 
- When called, all of the dice's are revealed and the total amount of a face is counted
- If there are less copies of the dice taht the previous player bid for, the previous player
loses the round

Extra:
- Dice face of 1 is wild and counts for every dice
